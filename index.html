The Akan Naming System.

The Akan naming system comes from the traditional people of countries such as Togo, Ivory Coast and Ghana.
Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture, and children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Here are Ghanian day names:

Day of
the week.		 Male. Female.
Sunday Kwasi Akosua
Monday Kwadwo Adwoa
Tuesday Kwabena Abenaa
Wednesday Kwaku Akua
Thursday Yaw Yaa
Friday Kofi Afua
Saturday Kwame Ama
